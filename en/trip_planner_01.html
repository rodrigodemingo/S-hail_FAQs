How can I plan my journey?

Let me help you plan your journey! You start with "Trip Planning". Enter your start point (next to "A") and your destination (next to "B"). Do you want to leave right now, or sometime later?

Click on "Departure now" to change the time you want to depart or arrive. Click on "Search" to start searching for possible options.

Now, you can see the "Search Results". By default, you see a list of all the public transport options.

If you want to take a different transport mode (e.g. taxi), you can click on the icon on the top of the screen.
