Marine Services

Assistance
  1. The crew assists the People of Determination to enter water bus easily & an employee is deployed at each marina.
  2. LCD monitors are available to provide information about waterbus, and safety procedures.
Accessibility
  1. All marine transport stations have been designed to be user-friendly to the People of Determination.
  2. Water Bus can be used by the People of Determination; it is designed to ease their movement.
  3. Providing ample seating areas on board the waterbus for the People of Determination by accommodating up to 3 wheelchairs.
  4. The Ferry has been designed to be used by all community segments, including the People of Determination.
  5. Suitable spaces have been provided for 2 wheelchairs in Ferry Dubai.
  6. Seats are 20% foldable to meet the requirements of the People of Determination through the provision ample space for the entry & exit of wheelchairs, thus ensuring stability on board.
