Taxi

Customers can call the Taxi booking and dispatch Dubai Taxi Tel +9714280808 (24 hours a day, 7 days a week), where the booking officer will enter the details in the system and direct the nearest available taxi to the customer location.

Assistance
  1. Operating 5 taxis to meet the needs of the People of Determination 24 hours a day covering all areas of Dubai and driven by well-trained drivers.
Health & Safety
  1. These vehicles are fitted with:
    1. Special lifts for the wheelchairs.
    2. Artificial respiratory systems.
    3. A wheelchair on board.
    4. Seats for the companions.
Special Fare
  1. Revising the fare system applicable to the People of Determination such that it charges on the same basis as the normal taxis in Dubai.
