How can I store trips as favorites?

If you do a trip very often, you can save it as a favorite. When you go to the first screen (Trip planning) and select "Recent Searches,” your recent searches will appear on top of the list and you only need to click on it to get trips starting now.

To mark a trip as a favorite, you can either click on the star next to the trip in your “Recent Searches” list or search for a trip and give it a star (top right corner).
